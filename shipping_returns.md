# Shipping & Returns Policy
- Standard shipping: 2–5 days
- Express shipping: 1–3 days
- Made-to-order: 10–14 days
- Returns: 14 days within delivery, unworn, original condition
- Refunds: 3–5 days; Exchanges: 5–7 days

## Shipping Details (clarifications)
- Handling time: orders placed before 2pm local time ship same day (Mon–Fri); after 2pm ship next business day.
- Express shipping: 1–3 days in transit; PO Boxes not eligible; certain rural areas may require +1 day.
- International (if enabled): duties/taxes may be charged on delivery depending on destination.

## Returns – Exceptions & Tips
- Final sale or hygiene-sensitive categories (e.g., lingerie) are not returnable unless defective.
- Try-on marks or detached tags can delay approval; we may request photos for pre-approval.

## Refund/Exchange Processing Clarifiers
- Refund speed varies by issuer; we submit within 3–5 business days after the return is scanned by carrier.
- Exchanges ship once the original is in transit (label scanned) to speed things up.

## Immediate Options (customer retention)
- Immediate Store Credit (keep item, 100% store credit instantly).
- Immediate Partial Refund (keep item, 50% cash back immediately).
- These options may vary by account/order history.
