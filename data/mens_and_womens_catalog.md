# Catalog Coverage
Style Loom offers apparel for **men and women**.

## Men – Primary Categories
- shirts (long-sleeve, short-sleeve, tees)
- pants (chino, tailored, jogger)
- jackets (lightweight, everyday)
- knitwear (crew, cardigan)
- sleepwear and active basics

## Women – Primary Categories
- blouses, dresses, skirts
- knitwear (City Knit), Everyday Jacket
- Tailored Pants, Weekend Dress
- t-shirts, activewear, sleepwear

## Language Cues
- “for a man”, “mens”, “men’s”, “male” ⇒ treat as men’s catalog context
- If user is ambiguous, ask: “Which category would you like—shirts, pants, jackets, or knitwear?”
